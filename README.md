# **Ridevest**

Last mile logistics in Africa fails too often, with 25-30% of deliveries
unsuccessful at the first attempt. This is due to poor addressing, unreliable
tracking systems, and error-prone proof-of-delivery methods.

Ridevest helps logistics companies reduce failed deliveries by providing a
reliable tracking system, smarter addressing, and foolproof proof of delivery
methods.

### How It Works

*Ridevest utilizes the Traccar app for reliable, accessible, and inexpensive*
*geolocation.*

1. **Company Onboarding:**

   - Logistics companies sign up and access a dashboard
   - Riders are onboarded and assigned to the company
   - Each rider is linked to a Traccar Client app downloaded already on their
     delivery device.

2. **Order Creation:**

   - Companies receive a shareable order link for SMEs and vendors
   - Orders are created and assigned to available riders

3. **Real-Time Delivery Tracking:**

   - Both the business and customer track deliveries live
   - System provides continuous updates:
     - Location tracking
     - Speed and movement insights
     - Delivery status updates

4. **Proof of Delivery** _(Foolproof System)_:

   - At drop-off:
     - Rider captures photo evidence
     - Customer confirms with a tracking code (OTP)
     - GPS location is recorded

5. **Payment & Escrow:**

   - Delivery fees are held securely in escrow
   - Funds are only released after successful delivery confirmation

6. **Payout**
   - Once delivery is verified, funds are released to the logistics company
