# **Ridevest**

Last mile logistics in Africa fails too often, with 25-30% of deliveries
unsuccessful at the first attempt. This is due to poor addressing, unreliable
tracking systems, and error-prone proof-of-delivery methods.

Ridevest helps logistics companies reduce failed deliveries by providing a
reliable tracking system, smarter addressing, and foolproof proof of delivery
methods.
